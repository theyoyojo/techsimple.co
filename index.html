About Us

We are a team of young adults in the Boston area who have grown up using modern technology. Based on those years of experience, we have learned how to solve our own technology problems and helped family members many times with their own technology issues.

Our goal for TechSimple is to spread those skills to all those that wish to learn how to use modern computer technology.
